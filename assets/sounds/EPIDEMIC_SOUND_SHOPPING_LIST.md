## Tips for Epidemic Sound

1. Use filters: Set duration to "0-30 seconds" for SFX
2. Look in "Sound Effects" section, not "Music" for SFX
3. For BGM, check the "Stems" option for cleaner loops
4. Download as MP3, 192kbps is fine for mobile
5. After downloading, rename files exactly as listed above
